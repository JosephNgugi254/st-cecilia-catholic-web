Outstations of st cecilia catholic church Isinya

1. St Mark's Ilpolosat

St. Mark's Catholic Church, established in 1999, is a vibrant faith community that started under the guidance of St. Monica Kitengela Parish. Nestled on a serene 2-acre plot, our church serves as a spiritual home for six dedicated families, comprising nearly twenty Christians in total.
Our church grounds feature the church building, two toilets, and a kitchen area, all designed to support our community activities and gatherings. Additionally, we are home to the St. Mary's Small Christian Community, fostering closer bonds among our members. Our church leadership is committed to the spiritual growth and well-being of our congregation.
