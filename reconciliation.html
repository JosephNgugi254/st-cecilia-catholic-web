RECONCILIATION

The Sacrament of Reconciliation, also known as Confession, holds a central place in our spiritual life. This sacrament offers individuals the opportunity to seek forgiveness for their sins and reconcile with God and the Church. During the Sacrament of Reconciliation, individuals are invited to examine their conscience, confess their sins to a priest, and receive absolution. Through heartfelt contrition and sincere repentance, believers experience the healing grace of God's mercy and are restored to a state of grace.

At St. Cecilia Catholic Church, we encourage all members of our faith community to avail themselves of the Sacrament of Reconciliation regularly, as it provides spiritual nourishment and strengthens our relationship with God. Through the sacrament, we encounter the boundless love and forgiveness of our Lord, enabling us to grow in holiness and live more fully the Christian life..
