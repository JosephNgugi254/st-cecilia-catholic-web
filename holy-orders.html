HOLY ORDERS

The Sacrament of Holy Orders holds profound significance as a sacred ritual through which men are ordained to serve the Church as deacons, priests, or bishops. This sacrament is revered as a divine calling and commitment to ministry within our faith community. Within the Sacrament of Holy Orders, candidates undergo a thorough process of discernment, formation under the guidance of the church
