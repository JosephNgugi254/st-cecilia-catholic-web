PONTIFICIAL MISSIONARY CHILDHOOD (PMC)



Pontifical Missionary Childhood abbreviated as PMC is a catholic lay movement whose main goal is to enhance the spiritual growth of Catholic Women and enable them participate more effectively in the evangelization and sanctification of humanity.

   Vision

To be strong in faith

   Mision

To seek and bear witness to Christ, committed to holistic development of Catholic women and families in the parish.

   Projects and Activities

The CWA is a very a very vibrant group that conducts activities according to its National Calendar. Over the years, the CMA has conducted a number of activities that have been well appreciated throughout the parish in conjunction with the parish council

   How to become a member:

It is open to all Catholic women of 18 years and above (married or single) who are parishioners at St Cecilia.
