CATHOLIC WOMEN ASSOCIATION (CWA)



The Catholic Women's Association (CWA) is a dynamic and integral part of our parish community. Comprised of dedicated women who are committed to living out their faith in service to others. Founded on the principles of faith, fellowship, and service, the CWA provides a nurturing and supportive environment for women to grow spiritually, connect with one another, and make a positive impact in the world around them. Through prayer, study, and action, members of the CWA strive to deepen their relationship with God and live out the Gospel message in their daily lives. Our church has over a hundred members, with approximately 60 registered CWA members who are active participants.

   Vision

To be strong in faith

   Mision

To seek and bear witness to Christ, committed to holistic development of Catholic women and families in the parish.

   Activities

The group engages in numerous activities aimed at fostering spiritual growth and evangelization. These activities include monthly seminars and retreats. Additionally, the group has purchased tents that they lend out, a project they began to generate some income.

   Are you interested in becoming a member?

CWA warmly welcomes all women aged 18 and above, regardless of marital status, who are parishioners of St. Cecilia Catholic Church.
